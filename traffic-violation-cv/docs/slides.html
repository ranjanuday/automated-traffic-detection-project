Preprocessing
Image Preprocessing

Adaptive Enhancement for Any Condition

Keeps detection robust across challenging environments

Low Light

Gamma correction + CLAHE + denoise restore visibility in night scenes.

Rain & Haze

CLAHE + bilateral filtering cut through atmospheric noise.

Shadows

Local contrast equalization recovers detail in harsh shadow.

Motion Blur

Deblurring sharpens fast-moving vehicles and riders.

Optional deep restoration: Restormer de-rain and de-blur models are applied when conditions demand stronger recovery.
Detection
Vehicle & Road-User Detection

Sensor Fusion for Accurate Classification

Two complementary models combined for regional accuracy

COCO YOLOv8

Strong on universal classes: person, car, bus, truck, motorcycle, bicycle, and traffic light.

Indian-Vehicle YOLO

Adds auto-rickshaw & rickshaw classes and re-labels three-wheelers that COCO mislabels as "car".

Result: Fusion logic merges both detectors so each vehicle category is correctly classified — even in dense, mixed South-Asian traffic.

Two-Stage Automatic Number-Plate Recognition

Stage 1 — Detect

YOLOv5 plate detector locates and tight-crops the number plate from the full scene.

Stage 2 — Read

EasyOCR extracts the registration string from the cropped plate region.

Roadmap
Limitations & Future Work

Honest Boundaries, Clear Next Steps

Current Limitations

• Seatbelt model imperfect on out-of-distribution images

• Wrong-side from a still frame is advisory (needs video/motion)

• Small labelled eval set (needs hundreds of annotated images)

• Config-driven detectors (parking, divider, seatbelt) need per-camera setup

Future Work

• Video tracking for motion-based violations

• Larger detector backbones for higher accuracy

• Fine-tuned region-specific models

• Scalable production deployment
